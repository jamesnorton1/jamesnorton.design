About me

Currently I’m working as a UI/UX Designer at Soldo, one the Europe’s fastest-growing fintechs, where I'm helping to design features within the product team and scale our design system. I also offer freelance design and consultancy services.

Previously I worked at Ten4, a digital agency, consulting for clients including UK government departments, FTSE 250 companies, and multi-national organisations.

Outside of work, I like learning new skills and working on side projects. Currently, learning to create motion design and animation in After Effects and attempting to produce music in Logic.

Beyond sitting at a computer, I like seeing live music, climbing, and skateboarding.

If you'd like to know more or have any enquiries, please drop me an email.
